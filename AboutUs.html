Our Story

At Calibrate Motors, we believe that luxury is more than just a vehicle—it's an experience that begins the moment you step through our doors.

Redefining Luxury Car Experience

Founded in 2015, Calibrate Motors was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a luxury car dealership that prioritizes the client experience above all else. Our founders, with over three decades of combined experience in the automotive industry, recognized a gap in the market for a truly client-focused luxury car experience.

Today, we are proud to be recognized as one of the premier luxury automotive dealerships in Los Angeles, offering a handpicked selection of the world's most exceptional vehicles. Our state-of-the-art showroom is designed to showcase these masterpieces of engineering in an environment that reflects their prestige.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to personalized service. We believe that acquiring a luxury vehicle should be an exciting and memorable journey, not just a transaction. That's why every member of our team is dedicated to providing an experience that exceeds your expectations from the moment you walk through our doors.

Our Journey

The milestones that have shaped Calibrate Motors

2015

The Beginning

Calibrate Motors was founded with a vision to create a new standard in luxury car dealerships, opening our first boutique showroom in Los Angeles.

2017

Service Excellence

We expanded our offerings with a state-of-the-art service center, staffed by factory-trained technicians to maintain the highest standards of care for our clients' vehicles.

2019

Recognition & Growth

Calibrate Motors was recognized as one of the top luxury dealerships in California, allowing us to expand our showroom to accommodate a larger selection of premium vehicles.

2021

Digital Transformation

We launched our digital platform to enhance the client experience, allowing for virtual tours, online reservations, and personalized vehicle configurations.

2023

Luxury EV Leadership

Recognized our commitment to sustainable luxury by becoming one of the premier dealers of high-performance electric and hybrid vehicles in Southern California.

2025

Celebrating a Decade

Celebrating 10 years of excellence with the opening of our flagship experience center, featuring exclusive client amenities and an expanded inventory of the world's finest automobiles.
